#+title: v0.4 — Bring-Your-Own-Dataplane & Airgap Ergonomics

* Purpose

v0.1–v0.3 built the cluster kubenyx opinionatedly wants: zero-daemon
bridge networking, volatile datastores, declared membership. v0.4 makes
kubenyx a good HOST for clusters that want different opinions at the
dataplane and workload layer — a CNI that enforces NetworkPolicy and
encrypts, workloads that claim PersistentVolumes, airgap image sets in
standard archive formats — without kubenyx growing any of those
opinions itself. Every piece is a hand-off: kubenyx steps aside
cleanly, it does not reimplement.

The testing-first invariant continues to bind: the defaults are
untouched, and the single-node fast path must stay unit-identical.
