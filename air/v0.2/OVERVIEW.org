#+title: v0.2 — Instant Test Clusters

* Purpose

v0.2 pushes the testing-first mission (architecture.org, user steer 2026-07-05) past what full VMs can deliver: disposable Kubenyx clusters that appear in single-digit seconds via microVMs, with everything volatile by design.
