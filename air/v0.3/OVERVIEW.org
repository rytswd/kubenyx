#+title: v0.3 — Durable Clusters (draft)

* Purpose

v0.1 proved stock Kubernetes as NixOS services; v0.2 made disposable
clusters instant. v0.3 extends the same declared-membership model to
clusters that are allowed to *matter*: persistent state, an HA control
plane, and operator workflows — without taxing the testing-first fast
path (every HA branch is gated on =length servers > 1= and the
=balanced= profile; single-node testing boots stay byte-identical).

* Relationship to v0.2

The multi-node microVM mesh ([[file:../v0.2/multinode-microvm.org][multinode-microvm.org]]) and this milestone
share foundations, implemented once during the mesh work:
- =kubenyx.nodes.<name>.role= — membership knows the server set.
- The credential bundle format (one tar per node) — shipped over the
  operator channel here, over the tap handoff there.
- The endpoint abstraction — a single =controlPlaneEndpoint= string
  today; a local health-checking LB (or external VIP) here.
