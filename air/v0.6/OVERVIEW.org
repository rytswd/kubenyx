#+title: v0.6 — Flexible Testing Strategy

* Purpose

Kubenyx's modules were proven inside kubenyx's own test matrix. v0.6
turns the repo outward: make embedding a kubenyx cluster in *someone
else's* NixOS test harness a first-class, low-boilerplate operation.
The milestone is driven by a real external embedding exercise — a
large consumer test suite hosted kubenyx clusters inside its existing
NixOS VM tests — and everything that exercise had to hand-write or
work around lands here in generalized form.
